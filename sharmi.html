Rose Milk

A Refreshing & Sweet Summer Drink

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chilled milk
  • 2 tablespoons rose syrup (e.g., Rooh Afza or similar)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pour the chilled milk into a glass or jug.
  2. Add rose syrup and mix well until fully blended.
  3. Add sugar if extra sweetness is desired.
  4. Serve with ice cubes or chill further before serving.
  5. Garnish with rose petals (optional).
